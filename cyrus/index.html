Cyrus Motorsports AJCE is a students' formula racing team of Amal Jyothi College of Engineering established under Club TORERO. The website was developed during the month of March 2023 and had completed successfully with my team. TORERO, The Technical Club of Amal Jyothi College of Engineering, Kanjirapally is a collective of students and faculty of the college aimed to grant students the possibility to partake in multiple national and international events organized by expert associations like SAE (Society of Automotive Engineers) etc. The website leads through the history, formation, and achievements of the Cyrus Motorsports club.

Embarking on the journey of Cyrus Motorsports AJCE marks my first team endeavor, a project where I contributed alongside passionate peers in the pursuit of excellence. This project helped to learn and master the art of teamwork and collaboration, in order to become successful in team dynamics.

In the dynamic world of web development, we've harnessed the power of HTML , CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript to craft an immersive online experience. HTML forms the structural framework, CSS adds aesthetic flair, Bootstrap ensures responsiveness across devices, and JavaScript injects interactivity, creating a digital track where enthusiasts can explore our race towards excellence.
