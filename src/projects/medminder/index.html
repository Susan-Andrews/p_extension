Medminder - Miniproject

View the demo

Medminder is a pill reminder app designed to help users stay on track with their medication schedule. The app sends reminders to the user at the appropriate times, reminding them to take their medication according to the schedule with the help of a voice assistant. By using the app, users can reduce the risk of missed doses, which can lead to complications and treatment failure. On the other hand, it also helps the patient's bystanders or caretakers, especially in the case of aged and small children, to get notified whether they have taken the medicine or not.

In the collaborative team effort of four, I took a lead role in shaping the user interface for Medminder, contributing significantly to its visual appeal and usability. My responsibilities included conceptualizing and implementing key UI elements, ensuring an intuitive and engaging experience. With a focus on user-centric design, my contributions played a central role in transforming functional requirements into a seamless interface. The success of Medminder reflects our collective efforts, where my emphasis on UI design contributed to creating a visually compelling and user-friendly pill reminder application.

Medminder, a pill reminder app, is expertly crafted using Flutter and Dart, guaranteeing a smooth and visually appealing user experience. With dynamically designed pages, the app offers an intuitive interface for users to effortlessly navigate and interact. The integration of Firebase ensures secure data storage, allowing users to access their medication schedules seamlessly across devices. By sending timely reminders and utilizing a voice assistant, Medminder significantly reduces the risk of missed doses.
